The Practice

Wellness,
practiced privately.

Most programs hand you a plan and wish you well. Solmara begins the other way around, with a long, careful look at who you already are. Your habits and history. Your sleep, your seasons, your photographs. The way your weeks actually unfold, rather than the way a template assumes they do.

From that portrait, we compose a twelve-week protocol that belongs to you alone: nutrition, movement, restoration, and ritual, arranged around your real life and refined week by week with your concierge.

We accept thirty clients each season. Not because scarcity is fashionable, but because attention doesn't scale.

The Season

Three movements,
twelve weeks.

i. · weeks one to two

Attune

We listen first. Your intake, your photographs, and a private consultation become a portrait of where you are: the honest starting point every transformation deserves.

ii. · weeks three to nine

Restore

Your protocol unfolds in weekly chapters: nourishment, movement, and rest calibrated to your body and your calendar, adjusted in conversation with your concierge.

iii. · weeks ten to twelve

Become

The practices that served you become permanent. We close with a final portrait, a keepsake dossier, and a rhythm you can keep without us.

The Concierge Difference

What accompanied means.

i.

One voice, all season

A single dedicated concierge from first consultation to final portrait. No queues, no tickets, no re-explaining yourself.

ii.

Weekly recalibration

Life moves; your protocol moves with it. Each week is reviewed and re-composed around what actually happened, not what was supposed to.

iii.

Discretion as a discipline

Your photographs, answers, and progress live in a private dossier seen only by your concierge team, and are returned or destroyed at season's end, at your word.
