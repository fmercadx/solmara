The Atelier

Small on purpose.
Personal by design.

Solmara was founded on an unfashionable conviction: that wellness, done honestly, cannot be mass-produced.

Why an Atelier

The word is
the promise.

An atelier is a workshop where things are made one at a time, by hand, for a particular person. We chose the word because it is a standard, not a flourish. Nothing at Solmara is produced in batches: not the protocols, not the conversations, not the attention.

The wellness industry is very good at scale. Programs are templated, coaching is queued, and the person at the center becomes an entry in someone's dashboard. We built the opposite: thirty clients a season, one concierge per client, and a protocol that begins as a blank page every single time.

It is slower this way, and more expensive to run. It is also the only way we know to do the work honestly.

I.F.
The Founder

Isabel Ferrer

Isabel spent eleven years directing programs at destination spas on two continents, and left when she could no longer ignore what the intake spreadsheets were telling her: the guests who transformed were never the ones with the strictest plans. They were the ones somebody had actually listened to.

Solmara is that observation, built into a company. She still reads every intake herself.

The Four Disciplines

What we hold
ourselves to.

Privacy

Your dossier is seen by your concierge team and no one else. Photographs and records are returned or destroyed at season's end, at your written word. Discretion is not a feature; it is the floor.

Slowness

Twelve weeks is deliberate. Anything faster is a performance; anything slower loses the thread. We move at the pace at which change actually holds.

Precision

Every recommendation traces back to something you told us or showed us. If we cannot explain why a practice is yours, it does not enter your protocol.

Warmth

Rigor without warmth is just another regimen. Your concierge is on your side of the table; the season is composed with you, never applied to you.
